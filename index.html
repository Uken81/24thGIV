24th Georgia Volunteer Infantry

The Irish Legion

Welcome to the 24th Georgia Irish Volunteers of the Irish Legion. A community dedicated to playing War of Rights in structured, regimented events.

Formed on June 4, 2020 with Celtic pride, the 24th Georgia has become one of the premiere regiments in all of War of Rights.

The regiment hosts the very best artillery unit in the game, the fearsome lane's Battalion.

Grab your Columbiads & revolvers, join the Irish Legion today, and prepare to charge while shouting "Faugh-a-ballaugh!"
